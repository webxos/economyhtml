Common and Powerful Islamic Duas and Prayers

In the name of Allah Subhanahu wa Ta'ala (سبحانه وتعالى - Glorified and Exalted is He) and with blessings upon Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم (Peace Be Upon Him).

Arabic (العربية) Term/Name Definition English Pronunciation English Translation
رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ Rabbana Atina A comprehensive supplication asking Allah Subhanahu wa Ta'ala (سبحانه وتعالى) for goodness in this world and the Hereafter, and protection from Hellfire. From the Quran (Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:201). Rabbana atina fid-dunya hasanatan wa fil-akhirati hasanatan wa qina adhaban-nar Our Lord, give us in this world [that which is] good and in the Hereafter [that which is] good and protect us from the punishment of the Fire.
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الْجَنَّةَ وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ النَّارِ Dua for Jannah and Protection A powerful Dua seeking Paradise and refuge from Hellfire, reflecting the ultimate goal of a believer, as taught by Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم (Peace Be Upon Him). Allahumma inni as’aluka al-jannata wa a’udhu bika minan-nar O Allah, I ask You for Paradise and seek Your protection from the Fire.
لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ إِنِّي كُنْتُ مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ Dua of Yunus (AS) Recited by Prophet Yunus (Jonah) in the belly of the whale, this Dua is a plea for Allah Subhanahu wa Ta'ala’s (سبحانه وتعالى) mercy and forgiveness, known for relieving distress (Surah Al-Anbiya, 21:87). La ilaha illa anta subhanaka inni kuntu minadh-dhalimeen There is no deity except You; exalted are You. Indeed, I have been of the wrongdoers.
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي ظَلَمْتُ نَفْسِي ظُلْمًا كَثِيرًا وَلَا يَغْفِرُ الذُّنُوبَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ فَاغْفِرْ لِي مَغْفِرَةً مِنْ عِنْدِكَ وَارْحَمْنِي إِنَّكَ أَنْتَ الْغَفُورُ الرَّحِيمُ Dua for Forgiveness A heartfelt supplication for forgiveness of sins, acknowledging human shortcomings and seeking mercy from Allah Subhanahu wa Ta'ala (سبحانه وتعالى), often recited after prayers. Allahumma inni zalamtu nafsi zulman kathiran, wa la yaghfiruz-zunuba illa Anta, faghfirli maghfiratan min ‘indika warhamni, innaka Antal-Ghafurur-Rahim O Allah, I have wronged myself greatly, and none forgives sins except You, so grant me forgiveness from You and have mercy on me, for You are the Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الَّذِي لَا يَضُرُّ مَعَ اسْمِهِ شَيْءٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَلَا فِي السَّمَاءِ وَهُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ Dua for Protection A morning and evening Dua taught by Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم (Peace Be Upon Him) for protection from harm, invoking Allah’s Subhanahu wa Ta'ala (سبحانه وتعالى) all-hearing and all-knowing attributes. Bismillahilladhi la yadurru ma’asmihi shay’un fil-ardi wa la fis-sama’i wa huwas-Sami‘ul-‘Alim In the name of Allah with whose name nothing is harmed on earth nor in the heavens, and He is the All-Hearing, the All-Knowing.
اللَّهُمَّ رَبَّنَا أَنزِلْ عَلَيْنَا مَائِدَةً مِّنَ السَّمَاءِ تَكُونُ لَنَا عِيدًا لِّأَوَّلِنَا وَآخِرِنَا وَآيَةً مِّنكَ ۖ وَارْزُقْنَا وَأَنتَ خَيْرُ الرَّازِقِينَ Dua for Sustenance A supplication from the Quran (Surah Al-Ma’idah, 5:114) asking Allah Subhanahu wa Ta'ala (سبحانه وتعالى) for provision and blessings, symbolizing trust in His sustenance. Allahumma Rabbana anzil ‘alayna ma’idatan minas-sama’i takunu lana ‘idan li-awwalina wa akhirina wa ayatan minka, warzuqna wa anta khayrur-raziqin O Allah, our Lord, send down to us a table [spread with food] from the heaven to be for us a festival for the first of us and the last of us and a sign from You. And provide for us, and You are the best of providers.
حَسْبِيَ اللَّهُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ وَهُوَ رَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيمِ Hasbiyallah A powerful Dua affirming reliance on Allah Subhanahu wa Ta'ala (سبحانه وتعالى) alone, often recited for strength and trust in times of difficulty (Surah At-Tawbah, 9:129). Hasbiya Allahu la ilaha illa huwa ‘alayhi tawakkaltu wa huwa Rabbul-‘arshil-‘azim Allah is sufficient for me; there is no deity except Him. On Him I have relied, and He is the Lord of the Great Throne.
اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَىٰ سَيِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدٍ وَعَلَىٰ آلِ سَيِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدٍ Salawat on Prophet Muhammad A supplication sending blessings upon Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم (Peace Be Upon Him), encouraged in Quran (Surah Al-Ahzab, 33:56) for spiritual reward and closeness to Allah Subhanahu wa Ta'ala (سبحانه وتعالى). Allahumma salli ‘ala Sayyidina Muhammadin wa ‘ala ali Sayyidina Muhammad O Allah, send blessings upon our master Muhammad and upon the family of our master Muhammad.
